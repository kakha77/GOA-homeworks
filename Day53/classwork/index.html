Simple Omelette Recipe

As easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil to a pan and place over medium heat.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour the eggs into the pan and tilt to spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: When the eggs begin to set, add your fillings on one side.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette in half and serve hot.
